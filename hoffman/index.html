At Hoffmans you will find many types of European style cakes for
special events, celebrations, and weddings.

We specialize in customizing birthday cake ideas and making our customers' wedding cake ideas a reality. We also offer a large selection of breakfast pastries, cookies and small desserts. Hoffman’s serves savory light lunches of sandwiches, homemade soups, salads and quiche. We offer some of the best sandwiches in Redmond!

Our products are freshly baked everyday and we use only the highest quality ingredients. The flour we use for our breads and pastries is locally sourced and sustainably farmed.

Hoffman’s Fine Cakes and Pastries was established in December 1984 in downtown Kirkland and moved to the city of Redmond in 2016.
